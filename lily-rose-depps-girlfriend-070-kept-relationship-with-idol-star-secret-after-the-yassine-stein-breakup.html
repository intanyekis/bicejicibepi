How Did Lily-Rose Depp And 070 Shake Meet?

Fans never found out the reason for Lily Rose-Depp and Timothee Chalamet's split. But every time Lily-Rose Depp is in a new relationship, fans discover the news through photos or public appearances.

According to People, Lily-Rose Depp posted about 070 Shake, who has been part of the music industry for a little while, on Instagram Stories in May 2023 and wrote “4 MONTHS W MY CRUSH.” The couple kissed, making it clear that they were together. Several publications took note when Depp wished 070 Shake a happy birthday in June 2023 and wrote "Happy birthday love of my life."

There have been countless photos of Lily-Rose Depp and 070 Shake. They have been pictured walking around New York City with coffee and also at Paris Fashion Week.

Everyone wants to know how the couple met, but unfortunately, neither Depp nor 070 Shake has shared that story. And they haven't mentioned their relationship in interviews, either. Depp has talked about how much she cares about her girlfriend on Instagram, but that's about it.

It's possible that Lily-Rose Depp and 070 Shake met at a party or perhaps because a mutual friend thought they would like each other. Or maybe they followed each other on Instagram and began chatting. While fans would love to know, it doesn't seem that the couple wants to share this information with the public.

Why Is Lily-Rose Depp And 070 Shake's Relationship So Private?

Lily-Rose Depp was interviewed by Elle in 2022 and said that she isn't used to talking about her personal life all the time. She said, “I am here to do my job, and what I really want to put out into the world is my work.”

Depp also said, "But I’ve just been raised in a manner that has taught me that privacy is something that’s important to protect."

Depp described herself as an "open book" around her pals, which suggests that they know more about her relationship with 070 Shake than the public does.

While Depp has dated many celebrities in the past, it seems that she has tried to keep all of them as secret as possible. She didn't give long interviews about Austin Butler or Timothee Chalamet. It makes sense that she hasn't talked about 070 Shake besides a few Instagram posts.

Even though Depp has been a known figure since childhood, she doesn't seem to think of herself as a celebrity. When interviewed by Entertainment Weekly, she said that she and her The Idol character Jocelyn aren't exactly the same.

Depp said, "I mean, my parents definitely did their best to give my brother and I the most 'normal' childhood that we could have. Obviously, still not totally normal, but a sense of normalcy at least, and a sense of childhood and freedom and play and everything. So our childhoods are quite different."

Depp continued to Entertainment Weekly, "Of course, experiencing this industry from a young age, there's obviously pieces of that that help me understand maybe Jocelyn's perspective a little bit better, but still, definitely coming from different places."

Because Depp says that she had a "normal childhood," she might be trying to live a regular life as much as possible. And that means not talking about dating 070 Shake all that much.

Just like Lily Rose-Depp doesn't always announce a new relationship right away, she doesn't seem to like talking about her break-ups, either.

Before she started seeing 070 Shake, Depp was in a relationship with the rapper Yassine Stein. Fans aren't sure why they broke up, though. When a relationship has run its course, Depp seems to move on privately. And since she posted about dating 070 Shake when they had been together for four months, it seems that she likes to see how a relationship is going before making it public.
