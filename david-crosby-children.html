David Crosby Children:- David Crosby who was also known as David Van Cortlandt Crosby, was an American singer, songwriter, and guitarist. David Crosby had 6 children during his life. Here’s what to know about everyone, amid news of his death.

David Crosby Children 

David Crosby was well known as a founding member of Crosby, Stills, & Nash. The legendary Singer passed away on 19 January 2023 at the age of 81.

David had 6 children, including 2 with fellow rocker Melissa Etheridge, and their names are James Raymond, Erika Keller Crosby, Donovan Crosby, Django Crosby, Bailey Jean Cypher, and Beckett Cypher.

As the world is in grief and the loss of one of rock’s most promising and enduring names, here’s what to know about each of his children.

James Raymond – 1962

David Crosby was 21 years old when his first child was born, a son named James Raymond. James Raymond was with Celia Crawford Ferguson in 1962. They both decided to send him for adoption, though James and his biological father were later reunited.

James is a musician and James and David performed together in the band CPR (Crosby, Pevar, & Raymond). 

Erika Keller Crosby – 1966

Erika Keller Crosby was born to David Crosby and Jackie Guthrie, and she has lived a private life out of the spotlight, so her age and date of birth are not known.

However, it is known that she has 4 siblings on her mother Jackie Guthrie’s side, and their father is a folk singer Arlo Guthrie. Erika Keller Crosby has 3 kids and they live in Florida.

Donovan Crosby

David’s 3rd child is a daughter named Donovan Crosby. She was with Debbie Donovan. Very little is known about Donovan Crosby, though the rock legend David did briefly address her as well.

Django Crosby – 1994

The musician David and Jan Dance married in 1987 and welcomed their son Django Crosby sometime in the mid-1980s. Django Crosby lives with David and he was by his side when David died.

It was Django’s mother Jan Dance who declared the rock star’s death in a statement David was surrounded by his wife Jan Dance and his son Django Crosby.

Bailey Jean Cypher – 1997

David Crosby’s youngest 2 children were sired by him, though he didn’t raise them. Melissa Etheridge and her partner Julie Cypher admitted to David being the sperm donor for their 2 children.

Bailey Jean Cypher was the first of those 2 children who were born in 1997 and is now about 25 years old.

On 19 January 2023, Bailey Jean Cypher took to Instagram to pay tribute to her biological father.

Beckett Cypher – 1998

Like Bailey Jean Cypher, Beckett Cypher was born to Melissa Etheridge and Julie Cypher in 1998, with David Crosby as the sperm donor. However, Beckett Cypher’s life ended tragically when he died due to opioid addiction in May 2020 at the age of just 21.
