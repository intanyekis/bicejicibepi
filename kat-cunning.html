Katrina Cunningham professionally known as Kat Cunning is the multi-talented entertainment personality who is grabbing wide media attention from her incredible dancing, acting, singing, and modeling, skills. Cunning has starred as Sabine in the Netflix series Trinkets.

The bold and beautiful Kat was born on November 12, 1992, in Oregon, USA, under the zodiac sign of Scorpio. She is the daughter of Chris Ann Cunningham (mother) and her husband. She has a brother named Robbie Cunningham.

Concerning education, Kat earned a Bachelor's of fine arts in dance from the State University of New York at Purchase (SUNY Purchase).  Cunning also trained at Classical Ballet Academy.
