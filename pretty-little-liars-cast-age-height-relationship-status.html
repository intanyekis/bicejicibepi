The small town of Rosewood may have pretty houses, chic coffee shops, and stylish residents, but for Alison DiLaurentis and her best friends, darkness is always present. The juicy plotlines on teen drama Pretty Little Liars never stopped for even one moment throughout all seven seasons, and fans still love it so much.

The PLL cast has made very positive comments about their time in Rosewood, and it's fun to learn more about the main cast of this popular show. From how old they are to how tall they are to what their love life is like, there are all kinds of details that PLL viewers can find out about.

10 Ashley Benson

Pretty Little Liars wouldn't be the same without Ashley Benson, who played Hanna Marin, the fashion-obsessed teenage girl who rolls her eyes often but cares deeply about her friends and family.

Benson is 30 years old and her birthday is December 18th. The actress is 5'5. As for her relationship status, she's dating G-Eazy, a rapper, according to The Daily Mail. This past spring 2020, Benson and her former partner, Cara Delevingne, broke up.

9 Lucy Hale

Playing smart and creative Aria Montgomery, Lucy Hale is another main cast member on Pretty Little Liars. Her romance with Ezra Fitz was the target of criticism, as he was her teacher.

Hale just celebrated her 31st birthday on June 14th. According to IMDb.com, the actress is 5'2.  What is Lucy Hale's love life like? Colton Underwood seems to be her new boyfriend, according to Teen Vogue. Sources told the publication that they are going on "casual dates".

8 Troian Bellisario

Troian Bellisario's portrayal of Spencer Hastings is truly something special. She brought a lot to the character, who is intelligent and serious. Bellisario is a bit older than some of her co-stars as she's 34 years old, and her birthday is October 28th. According to IMDb.com, she's taller than some of her co-stars as well at 5'7.

As for her relationship status, she married Patrick J. Adams in December 2016. They got married in California and have a baby daughter, Aurora.

7 Shay Mitchell

Shay Mitchell is beloved for her role on Pretty Little Liars as Emily Fields, who loves swimming and is one of the sweetest characters on the show.

The actress turned 33 on April 10th, and as for her height, IMDb.com states that she's 5'7 and 1/2. Shay Mitchell has a partner named Matte Babel. The two aren't married and have a baby girl named Atlas.

6 Ian Harding

Although Ian Harding's character Ezra Fitz is a teacher and much older than the rest of the characters, in real life he's the same age as Shay Mitchell.

Harding will turn 34 on September 16th, and IMDb.com puts his height at 5'11. His personal life is a bit of a mystery, as he isn't talked about as much as some of the other actors. Harding is in a serious relationship with Sophie Hart, a photographer, and Distractify.com says they started dating in 2011.

5 Tyler Blackburn

Even celebs love Pretty Little Liars and it's fair to say that Tyler Blackburn's character Caleb Rivers is one big reason why. As soon as he appears onscreen, he falls for Hanna, and the two have a truly fascinating love story as they can't stay away from one another but they have some issues.
