Gigi Hadid's Net Worth Outshines Her Sister Bella's, But How Much Does She Actually Make Modeling?

June 2024 · 4 minute read

The modeling industry has certainly become known for being challenging. However, it has also launched many young girls into a world of fame and fortune, with some of the best-known models in the industry including Kate Moss, Kendall Jenner, Miranda Kerr, Adriana Lima, Hailey Bieber, Bella Hadid, and of course, Gigi Hadid.

Both young girls have built incredibly successful modeling careers. Gigi was signed to IMG Models in 2013, before making her debut in the Top 50 Models on Models.com later in 2014. Over the course of her career, she has appeared over thirty-five times on international Vogue magazine covers, which is a huge privilege within itself for any model within the industry. Her sister is also incredibly successful within the modeling field, having made a total of twenty-seven appearances on international Vogue magazine covers. She has also appeared on the cover of several other publications. While building such a successful career, many fans have been left wondering exactly how much Gigi Hadid is worth.

Gigi Hadid Grew Up In A Wealthy Family

While Gigi has become best-known among the public for her reputable supermodel status, they may be unaware that she also comes from a wealthy family background. Gigi's mother, Yolanda, also built a successful modeling career for herself after she was asked by Dutch designer Frans Molenaar to replace one of his models at a show.

It was at this show that she was discovered and consequently signed a contract with Ford Models. She modeled for a total of fifteen years, before moving to LA where she met her now ex-husband Mohamed Hadid.

Mohamed Hadid also had an incredibly lucrative career as a prominent real estate developer, meaning that when the pair divorced, Yolanda was left with a small fortune.

While they were together, they also starred in Season 3 of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills, quickly making themselves well-known to the public.

A couple of years after starring on the show, Yolanda even landed her own reality show called Making a Model with Yolanda Hadid.

Gigi Hadid Has An Estimated Net Worth Of $29 Million

Coming from a family with a modeling background, it only seemed natural for the two daughters of the family to follow the same path. Since landing her first modeling gig at just two years old, Gigi has gone on to become one of the highest-paid supermodels in the industry, earning an estimated $9 million to $10 million per year.

So how much is Gigi Hadid actually worth? According to Celebrity Net Worth, the prolific supermodel has an estimated net worth of around $29 million, much of which likely comes from her modeling career.

Throughout the course of her career, she has landed deals with big-name brands within the industry such as BMW, Versace, Tommy Hilfiger, Maybelline, and Evian. She also models in Victoria's Secret Shows, which can earn her up to $1 million per year.

Thanks to her large Instagram following, Gigi also more than likely earns a significant amount from brand deals and endorsements. She even launched how own makeup collection in collaboration with Maybelline, which featured palettes and matte lipsticks.

Her sister, Bella Hadid, also has a similar net worth of an estimated $25 million. She, too, has built a highly successful modeling career, working with many high-profile brands and landing major brand deals. This makes Gigi and her sister two of the highest-paid models in the modeling world.

Both sisters have money in various properties and real estate. Some of Gigi's properties include two apartments in New York, which she reportedly combined into one, coming to an estimated total of over $9 million.

Other Models Also Have A Large Net Worth Like The Hadid Sisters

While both Gigi and Bella are considered some of the highest-paid models within the industry, there are also plenty of others who have reaped a fortune from the industry. Another one of the highest-paid models is Kendall Jenner, who has an estimated net worth of $45 million.

However, this is a result of various streams of income, such as the family's reality show and brand deals. For her modeling career, it is likely that she earns a similar amount to the Hadid sisters, if not more, although no exact figure has been reported.
