Amala Ratna Zandile Dlamini, better known as Doja Cat, was born in Los Angeles but moved to New York with her mother at a young age. After learning different dance forms as a child, she also learned Indian classical dances when her family practiced Hinduism. She joined troupes to focus on her breakdancing career and left high school after her interest in music began growing. Her music on SoundCloud slowly began to generate traction, and she earned a breakthrough with her second album in 2019, Hot Pink. Due to her mainstream success in 2020, Doja Cat has been a fixture on the red carpet, arriving at various events with her signature bold and beautiful outfits.

While working with celebrity stylist Brett Alan Nelson, Doja Cat has found her signature style with sheer gowns, feathered skirts, and sparkling accessories that make her shimmer in front of the cameras. Often named one of the best-dressed celebrities at events, let’s look at the most expensive red-carpet outfits worn by Doja Cat.

2021 MTV VMAs

While hosting the 2021 MTV VMAs, Doja Cat served with a series of fashionable looks, each different from the other. According to People, she wore a Vivienne Westwood black bustier dress from Andreas Kronthaler’s collection on the red carpet. The outfit was matched with a pair of thigh-high stockings under five-inch red platform heels. She also switched to another Vivienne Westwood outfit and a Thom Browne ensemble during the show.

2022 Daily Front Row Awards

Doja Cat came to collect her Breakthrough Fashion Icon award at Daily Front Row dressed in a power suit that exuded her bold personality. Her outfit consisted of a pinstriped suit from the 2022 Viktor & Rolf Fall/Winter collection. The singer completed her look with a pair of red Steve Madden shoes, diamond jewels, and a Puppets and Puppets cookie handbag.

2020 Billboard Music Awards

In one of her early red carpet appearances, Doja Cat fashioned long orange braids with the rest of her outfit. Her sheer Georges Hobeika dress was designed with crystals and a see-through look. The singer wore simple jewels from Lorraine Schwartz and Amina Muaddi lavender stilettos.

2021 Grammy Awards

When Doja Cat decided she wanted an ‘out there’ look for her appearance at the 2021 Grammys, she went nowhere else but Roberto Cavalli for her debut red carpet at the award show. Cat wore a fitted motorcycle jacket with an open zip attached to a green feathered fishtail gown, as noted by Buzzfeed. Her dark make-up and mullet accentuated her rocking get-up.

2020 iHeart Music Awards

Along with being named one of the best-dressed celebrities at the iHeart Music Awards, Doja Cat gave fans two different looks in one evening. She arrived in a green see-through Brandon Maxwell gown with a cape-style back and a high neckline.

2022 Billboard Music Awards

Doja Cat made every head turn in her Schiaparelli couture gown in 2022. As mentioned by Vogue. Her outfit was taken from the Daniel Roseberry-designed Spring 2022 collection. The aerodynamic neckline was enhanced with a black velvet bodice with a sheer chest. Her maxi skirt featured a sheer silk train for an added touch. Her shoes, clutch, and jewelry were also a Roseberry creation.

2021 Billboard Music Awards

Doja Cat went to the Balmain archives when she wanted to choose a look for the 2021 Billboard Music Awards. She donned a black-and-white striped ensemble with an off-the-shoulder bandeau top and flared high-waist pants. She also finished her outfit with a large turquoise pendant and silver earrings.

2020 AMAs

The singer wore another stunning Vivienne Westwood creation in an asymmetric strapless dress. The taupe dress featured a dipped neckline along with an electric blue trim. Her eye-catching 5-inch heels were also a Westwood creation with a lace-up front and a crocodile effect. The heels had a retail price of $1,440.

2022 Grammy Awards

Doja Cat was nominated for eight whopping Grammys at the 2022 ceremony, and she showed up in style to collect her awards. The singer chose a sea green Atelier Versace bodysuit with a see-through skirt, as stated by Cosmopolitan. The corset and gown were covered in Swarovski crystals, and she accessorized them with a Coperni glass handbag.

2022 Billboard Women In Music

Billboard Women In Music has been a special event each year, and Doja Cat made her contribution with an electric performance. She arrived on the red carpet in a chic black Carolina Herrera gown with a peplum skirt and a deep plunging V-neckline. Her look was completed with a chunky diamond neckline and chandelier diamond earrings.

Doja Cat has evolved her style over the years and slayed with every outfit she has worn at events. Working in many notable fashion houses, she has established herself as a trend-setting icon and continues to be unapologetically herself. The singer is working on her next studio album, which is expected to be released in early 2023.
