Who is the voice of Ryder in Paw Patrol?

June 2024 · 1 minute read

Keith Chapman and Guru Studio contributed the animation for the Canadian children’s television program PAW Patrol, which is produced by Spin Master Entertainment. The majority of the Canadian episodes of the series are broadcast on TVOntario as a part of the TVOKids programming block. August 2013 saw the debut of the program’s previews on TVO. On August 12, 2013, Nickelodeon in the US aired the series’ premiere.

The main character of the show is a little kid named Ryder who is in charge of the PAW Patrol, a group of canines that do search and rescue missions. They collaborate on missions to defend Adventure Bay, a coastal community, and its surroundings. Every dog has a unique set of abilities depending on emergency services careers, such as those of a firefighter, police officer, or pilot.

Who is the voice of Ryder in Paw Patrol?

In the PAW Patrol North American dub, Ryder was previously voiced by Jaxon Mercey. Late in Season 3, he took Elijha Hammill’s spot. As Ryder’s voice in Season 6, Joey Nijem took his position.
