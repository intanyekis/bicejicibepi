Anna Kendrick and Bill Hader break up: report

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Anna Kendrick and Bill Hader have apparently broken up.

The actors ended their nearly two-year relationship as quietly as they started it, “Entertainment Tonight” reported Tuesday.

When asked about the report, Hader’s rep told Page Six he had no comment.

Similarly, Kendrick’s rep told us, “It is my company’s policy not to comment nor to share information regarding our client’s personal life.”

While it is unclear when they officially got together, a source close to the couple revealed to People in January that they had been dating “for over a year.”

“They are both very private people, and with the pandemic, it was easy to keep it quiet,” the source added.

Kendrick, 36, and Hader, 44, met when she hosted “Saturday Night Live” in 2014. They later starred together in the 2019 Disney+ Christmas movie “Noelle.”

The only time either has made a comment about the other was when Hader shut down the possibility of Kendrick appearing on his TV series “Barry” during an interview with “ET” in April.

As for the reason they have kept their romance under wraps, the “It” star told the Hollywood Reporter he wants to protect the privacy of his three daughters with ex-wife Maggie Carey: Hannah, 12, Harper, 9, and Hayley, 7.

“[My daughters] just want me to be their dad,” he said in April. “They just want me to sit and watch ‘Encanto’ over and over and over again. So that’s what I do.”

Before Kendrick, Hader dated Rachel Bilson. They had sparked relationship rumors in late 2019 and made their red carpet debut at the 2020 Golden Globes, but they broke up later that same year.

As for Kendrick, who has kept her love life private in the past, she previously dated British cinematographer Ben Richardson after meeting on the set of “Drinking Buddies.”

While the “Pitch Perfect” star has never been married, she and Richardson, 46, sparked engagement rumors in 2015.
