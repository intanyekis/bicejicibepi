Who Is Cookie Johnson? Magic Johnson Wife Explored

Magic Johnson's wife, Cookie Johnson, is an activist and businesswoman who made her name in the clothing line.

Born and raised in Alabama, Cookie graduated with a degree in retailing and eventually used her knowledge to kick start her own career.

As she began in retailing, she relocated to Los Angeles and worked as a designer for a sportswear company.

Eventually, Cookie met Magic and got married in LA in 1991 and they are together ever since.

Just two months before the couple welcomed their first child, Magic was diagnosed with HIV and the couple is raising awareness about the disease actively since then.
